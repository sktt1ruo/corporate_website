Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU)

Description

The IMU products provide high-accuracy inertial measurement solutions to platform stabilization and pointing systems, high performance industrial applications, Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs), and Vehicles applications. The IMU integrates three-axis vibration-immunity gyroscopes, three-axis accelerometers, three-axis magnetometers, and a pressure sensor. The onboard processing includes attitude and heading reference, inertial navigation, Kalman and complementary filters, and dynamic compensation.
